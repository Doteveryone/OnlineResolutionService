How it works

The {{config.PROJECT_NAME}} is an independent service for reporting negative or harmful things online, such as harassment on social media, upsetting images or scams.

We'll explain your rights using information written by experts from different charities and support groups.

Then we'll guide you through the process of reporting your issue to the company responsible, or the authorities depending on the type of issue. We'll also send anonymous information to support groups to help them monitor the types of problems people are having online and to campaign for change.
