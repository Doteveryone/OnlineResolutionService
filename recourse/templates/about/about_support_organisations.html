Information for charities and support groups

The {{config.PROJECT_NAME}} helps charities and support groups meet the needs of the public using a shared platform. It also provides better data to organisations listed as 'super complainants' under the government's proposed online harms legislation.

Collaboratively written guidance

Our guides help explain people's rights and options for resolving issues they are having online. These guides are maintained collaboratively by charities and support organisations with different areas of expertise using GitHub.

Better data

We also send anonymous statistics to relevant charities and support groups to help them monitor the types of problems people are having online and to campaign for change.
